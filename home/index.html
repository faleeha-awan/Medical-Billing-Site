The Solution You Need

Simplify the complexities of healthcare billing through our all-in one medical billing and coding services, designed to improve claim accuracy and maximize revenue. Experience precision and compliance with our medical coding and billing solutions to enhance the RCM process of your practice.

Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)

We offer complete revenue cycle management, including claims submitting and follow-up, billing, and denials management. Our team optimizes all revenue cycle factors for optimal revenue and efficiency.

Claims Submission and Follow-Up

We submit claims accurately and on time to avoid denials and delays. Our staff follows up with payers to process and settle claims quickly, boosting your revenue.

Patient Billing and Collections

We handle all aspects of patient billing, including invoicing, payment processing, and collections. Our team ensures that all patient payments are processed efficiently.

Accurate Coding and Compliance

Our Certified coders provide accurate, up-to-date coding to reduce coding mistakes and denials. We also make sure all code meets laws to reduce compliance concerns.

Billing Denials Management

We have a track record of disputing denied claims, boosting revenue, and reducing write-offs. Our team examines denial patterns to find and fix revenue cycle problems.

Credentialing and Enrollment

We manage provider credentialing and enrollment processes so your practice can bill for all services. Our staff submits all required papers properly and on schedule, reducing enrollment delays and refusals.

MIPS & MACRA Consultation

We guide your practice through MIPS and MACRA reporting with personalized strategies tailored to value-based care. Our support helps you boost performance scores, maximize reimbursements, and stay compliant with changing regulations.

Medical Billing Audit

We conduct detailed audits to identify billing errors, uncover missed revenue opportunities, and ensure full compliance. Our expert reviews help improve accuracy and reduce denials across your revenue cycle.
